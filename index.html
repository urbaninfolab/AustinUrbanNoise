Urban Noise Map Information

About

This interactive map provides comprehensive urban noise information for Austin, Texas. The map includes:

  • Noise: Real-time urban noise data visualization
  • Points of Interest: Construction sites and other noise-generating activities
  • Traffic Flow: Live traffic conditions that contribute to urban noise levels

This map is part of the Austin Digital Twin project, providing real-time and historical noise data to help understand urban soundscape patterns in Austin.

Credits

This map was created by the Urban Info Lab at The University of Texas at Austin, as part of the Austin Digital Twin initiative under the Good Systems Initiative.

Additional support for this project comes from the Texas Advanced Computing Center (TACC).
